# What do Ethiopian Catholics do on Holy Thursday?

**Type:** qa
**Date:** 2026-04-09
**Question:** In Ethiopia today is the last Thursday of the Great Fast — what do Ethiopian Catholics do today?
**Last updated:** 2026-04-09
**Related:** [[liturgical/feasts/ጸሎተ-ሐሙስ]], [[teaching/eucharist]], [[teaching/passion-death-resurrection]], [[concepts/ምሥጢር]]

## Answer

Today (ሚያዝያ 1, 2018 ዓ.ም. / April 9, 2026) is **ጸሎተ ሐሙስ — Holy Thursday**, the day Jesus instituted the Eucharist at the Last Supper.

The Compendium teaches (Q272): ኢየሱስ ቁርባንን የመሠረተው፣ የመጨረሻውን እራት ከሐዋርያቱ ጋር በበላበትና «አልፎ በተሰጠበት ሌሊት» **ሐሙስ ዕለት** ነው።

### What Ethiopian Catholics do

1. **ቅዳሴ ሐሙስ (Holy Thursday Mass)** — The only Mass of the day, usually in the evening, commemorating the Last Supper. The priest re-enacts Jesus taking bread and wine: "ይህ ስለእናንተ የሚሰጥ ሥጋዬ ነው" — "This is my body given for you" (Q273).

2. **የእግር ማጠብ (Washing of Feet)** — The priest or bishop washes the feet of 12 people, following Jesus washing the apostles' feet (John 13). This is a major ceremony in Ethiopian Catholic and Orthodox tradition alike.

3. **ቅዱስ ቁርባን (Eucharistic Adoration)** — After Mass, the Blessed Sacrament is carried in procession to a side altar (altar of repose). The faithful stay to pray through the night, keeping watch with Jesus in Gethsemane.

4. **የአቡነ ዘበሰማያት ጸሎት (Extended prayer)** — The faithful meditate on the Passion. The Compendium (Q120) teaches: ኢየሱስ በሕማማቱ ዋዜማ ከሐዋርያቱ ጋር ባደረገው የመጨረሻ እራት ላይ ራሱን በፈቃደኝነት መሥዋዕት አድርጎ ማቅረቡን አስታወቀ — "On the eve of his Passion, at the Last Supper with his apostles, Jesus announced his free self-offering as sacrifice."

5. **ጾም continues** — The strict Lenten fast (no meat, no dairy, no food until afternoon) continues. Many fast completely until after the evening liturgy.

### Ethiopian Catholic vs Ethiopian Orthodox

Both celebrate essentially the same observances — foot washing, Last Supper commemoration, night vigil. The key difference: Ethiopian Catholics celebrate in communion with Rome and follow the Catholic understanding of the Eucharist as taught in the Compendium (Q274): **ቁርባን የክርስቲያናዊ ሕይወት ሁሉ ምንጭና ቁንጮ ነው** — "The Eucharist is the source and summit of all Christian life."

### The Triduum

Holy Thursday begins the **Sacred Triduum** (የሦስት ቀን ሐዘን):
- **ጸሎተ ሐሙስ** (Holy Thursday) — Institution of the Eucharist
- **ስቅለት** (Good Friday) — Crucifixion and Death
- **ቅዳሜ ስቡህ** (Holy Saturday) — Vigil and waiting
- **ፋሲካ** (Easter Sunday) — Resurrection
