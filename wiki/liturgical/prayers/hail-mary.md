## The prayer

> «ሰላም ለኪ ማርያም —
> ጸጋ የመላሽ፤
> ጌታ ካንቺ ጋር ነው።
> ከሴቶች መካከል አንቺ ብፅዕት ነሽ፤
> የማኅፀንሽም ፍሬ ኢየሱስ ብፁዕ ነው።
>
> ቅድስት ማርያም — የእግዚአብሔር እናት —
> ስለ እኛ ኃጢአተኞች ለምኚ —
> አሁንና በሞታችን ሰዓት።
> አሜን።»

## Source

The prayer has two halves:

**The Scriptural greeting** (the first half):
- *"ሰላም ለኪ ማርያም — ጸጋ የመላሽ — ጌታ ካንቺ ጋር ነው"* — the angel Gabriel's words at the Annunciation (ሉቃስ 1:28).
- *"ከሴቶች መካከል አንቺ ብፅዕት ነሽ፤ የማኅፀንሽም ፍሬ ኢየሱስ ብፁዕ ነው"* — Elizabeth's words at the Visitation (ሉቃስ 1:42).

**The Church's petition** (the second half):
- *"ቅድስት ማርያም — የእግዚአብሔር እናት — ስለ እኛ ኃጢአተኞች ለምኚ"* — added by the Church around the 13th–15th centuries, drawing on the Council of Ephesus (431 AD) which proclaimed Mary **Theotokos** (የእግዚአብሔር እናት).

## Why we pray it

We do not worship Mary — worship is for God alone. We **honor** her and **ask her intercession** because:

1. She is the **Mother of God** (Theotokos) — chosen to bear the Son of God in the flesh.
2. She is the **Mother of the Church** — given to John, and through him to all disciples, at the foot of the cross (ዮሐንስ 19:26–27).
3. She is **first among the saints** — full of grace from her Immaculate Conception, sinless throughout her life, assumed body and soul into heaven.
4. Her intercession at Cana (ዮሐንስ 2:1–11) shows she still speaks to her Son for our needs.

For Catholic apologetic on Marian devotion, see [[apologetics/mary-veneration]].

## How often to pray

- **In every decade of the Rosary** (10 Hail Marys + Our Father + Glory Be).
- **At the Angelus** three times a day (morning, noon, evening).
- **When facing temptation, danger, or grief** — Mary leads us to her Son.
