## What it is

The Rosary is a **meditation on the life of Christ through the eyes of his Mother**. Beads are simply a tool to keep count while the mind contemplates the mysteries. It is **scriptural prayer** — every mystery is drawn from the Gospels.

A complete Rosary is **20 mysteries** distributed across four sets, traditionally prayed on different days of the week.

## How to pray one set (5 mysteries / 5 decades)

Hold the crucifix and begin:

1. **Sign of the cross** + **Apostles' Creed**.
2. **Our Father** (on the first large bead).
3. **Three Hail Marys** (on the three small beads — for faith, hope, charity).
4. **Glory Be**.
5. **Announce the first mystery.** Briefly meditate on what it teaches. Then on the large bead pray **one Our Father**.
6. On the ten small beads, pray **ten Hail Marys** while continuing to meditate on the mystery. End with **one Glory Be**.
7. After each decade pray the **Fatima prayer**:
   > «ኢየሱስ ሆይ፣ ኃጢአታችንን ይቅር በለን፤
   > ከገሃነም እሳት አድነን፤
   > ሁሉንም ነፍሳት — በተለይም የአንተን ምሕረት የሚፈልጉትን — ወደ ሰማይ አግባቸው።»
8. Continue with the **second**, **third**, **fourth**, **fifth** mysteries the same way.
9. After the fifth decade pray the **Hail Holy Queen**:
   > «ሰላም ለኪ ንግሥት — የምሕረት እናት — የሕይወታችን — የጣፋጭነታችን — የተስፋችን — ሰላም።
   > የሔዋን ስደተኞች ልጆች — ወደ አንቺ እንጮኻለን።
   > በዚህ የእንባ ሸለቆ — እያኮሰሰን ወደ አንቺ እንለምናለን።
   > ስለዚህ — ጠባቂያችን — ምሕረትህ ዐይኖችሽን ወደ እኛ መልሺ።
   > ከዚህ ስደት በኋላ —
   > የማኅፀንሽ የተባረከ ፍሬ — ኢየሱስን አሳዪን።
   > ቸር — ርኅሩኅ — ጣፋጭ ድንግል ማርያም።
   > አሜን።»
10. **Sign of the cross** to conclude.

## The Four Sets of Mysteries (አራቱ የጸሎት ስብስቦች)

### 🟦 Joyful Mysteries — Mondays & Saturdays
*The mystery of God-with-us begins.*

1. **The Annunciation** (ሉቃስ 1:26–38) — Gabriel announces; Mary says "Yes."
2. **The Visitation** (ሉቃስ 1:39–56) — Mary visits Elizabeth; the Magnificat.
3. **The Nativity** (ሉቃስ 2:1–20) — Christ is born in Bethlehem.
4. **The Presentation in the Temple** (ሉቃስ 2:22–38) — Simeon's song.
5. **The Finding in the Temple** (ሉቃስ 2:41–52) — the Child Jesus among the teachers.

### 🟨 Luminous Mysteries — Thursdays
*Christ's public ministry — added by St. John Paul II in 2002.*

1. **The Baptism in the Jordan** (ማቴ. 3:13–17) — the Father's voice, the Spirit descending.
2. **The Wedding at Cana** (ዮሐ. 2:1–11) — water into wine; Mary intercedes.
3. **The Proclamation of the Kingdom** (ማር. 1:14–15) — call to repentance and faith.
4. **The Transfiguration** (ማቴ. 17:1–8) — Christ's glory revealed on the mountain.
5. **The Institution of the Eucharist** (ማቴ. 26:26–28) — the Last Supper.

### 🟥 Sorrowful Mysteries — Tuesdays & Fridays
*The Passion.*

1. **The Agony in the Garden** (ማቴ. 26:36–46) — "Not my will but yours."
2. **The Scourging at the Pillar** (ማቴ. 27:26).
3. **The Crowning with Thorns** (ማቴ. 27:27–31).
4. **The Carrying of the Cross** (ሉቃ. 23:26–32).
5. **The Crucifixion and Death** (ሉቃ. 23:33–46) — "It is finished."

### 🟪 Glorious Mysteries — Wednesdays & Sundays
*The triumph.*

1. **The Resurrection** (ማቴ. 28:1–10).
2. **The Ascension** (የሐዋ. ሥራ 1:9–11).
3. **The Descent of the Holy Spirit** (የሐዋ. ሥራ 2:1–4) — Pentecost.
4. **The Assumption of Mary** (Tradition) — body and soul into heaven.
5. **The Coronation of Mary** as Queen of Heaven (ራዕይ 12:1).

## Why the Rosary

- **It is biblical.** Every mystery is from the Gospels or Acts.
- **It is Christocentric.** Mary leads us to her Son. Every Hail Mary ends with the name of *Jesus*.
- **It is universal.** Children, scholars, the dying, the imprisoned have all prayed the same prayer for centuries.
- **Mary asks for it.** At Fatima (1917) and other apparitions, Mary repeatedly asked for the daily Rosary for peace.

## Tips for fruitful praying

- **Don't race.** Better to pray one decade well than five badly.
- **Use the mysteries as windows.** Don't worry if your mind wanders — gently return.
- **Pray with images** — an icon or picture of the mystery in front of you.
- **Pray in family** — even one decade together transforms a household.
- **Use a real rosary** — the beads in your hand keep the body engaged with the soul.
