## Biography

**ቅዱስ ይሁዳ (Saint Jude)** — also called **Thaddaeus** in Mark and Matthew — was an apostle, traditionally identified as a **kinsman of Jesus** ("brother of James" — ይሁዳ 1:1). The Gospels carefully distinguish him from **Judas Iscariot** the betrayer:

> «ይሁዳ — የአስቆሮት ሳይሆን።»
> — ዮሐ. 14:22

Tradition identifies him as the brother of **James the Less**, both sons of Mary (the wife of Clopas) — close relatives of Jesus though not literal siblings, since Mary the Mother of God remained ever-virgin.

Because his name was so similar to Judas Iscariot's, devotion to him was muted for centuries — many were reluctant to invoke a saint whose name resembled the betrayer's. This is why he became, by long tradition, the **patron of "impossible cases"** — the saint to whom one turns when all other helpers seem to have failed.

## The one Gospel question

In John 14, at the Last Supper, **Jude** asks Christ:

> «ጌታ ሆይ — ለምን ራስህን ለእኛ ብቻ — እንጂ ለዓለም — ለማታሳይ ሁነህ?»
> — ዮሐ. 14:22

Christ's answer is one of the great Trinitarian sayings:

> «ሰው ቢወደኝ — ቃሌን ይጠብቃል። አባቴም ይወደዋል — ወደ እርሱም እንመጣለን — ከእርሱም ጋር መኖሪያን እናደርጋለን።»
> — ዮሐ. 14:23

## The Letter of Jude

The brief but fiery **Epistle of Jude** is in the New Testament. It warns against false teachers who *"pervert the grace of God into licentiousness"* and contains the famous closing doxology:

> «ሊጠብቃችሁ … የሚችለው — እንዳትደፋዱ — እና በደስታ — በክብሩ ፊት — ያላንዳች ነውር — እንዳቀርባችሁ — ለሚችለው … ክብር ይሁነው።»
> — ይሁ. 24–25

## Mission and martyrdom

Tradition pairs Jude with **Simon the Zealot** in mission — they preached together in **Mesopotamia, Persia, and Armenia**. Both were martyred together — Jude by being clubbed to death, then beheaded with an axe (his iconographic symbol). Their feast is celebrated together in the Latin Church on October 28.

## Patron of impossible cases

The shrine of Saint Jude in Chicago and devotional centers worldwide receive enormous numbers of petitions from those facing despair — illness, addiction, broken families, financial ruin. The classic prayer:

> «ቅዱስ ይሁዳ ሐዋርያ — የጭንቀት ሰዓት ጓደኛ —
> በተስፋ መቁረጥ ጊዜ የሚረዳ —
> ለእኔ ስለዚህ ጥሪ — ስለዚህ ጭንቀት — ለምን።»

His patronage rests on a beautiful theological intuition: God reserves the **most desperate cases** for the saint whose name is shared with the betrayer — to teach that no situation, no name, no soul is beyond his reach.
