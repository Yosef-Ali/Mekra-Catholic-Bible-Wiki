## Why a daily routine?

ጸሎት የክርስቲያን ነፍስ ትንፋሽ ነው። እንደ ሥጋ ምግብ ሁሉ የተወሰኑ ጊዜዎች ቢኖሩትም፣ ቀኑን በሙሉ ከእግዚአብሔር ጋር መነጋገር የተሻለ ነው። ቤተክርስቲያን ለምእመናን **አምስት ቋሚ የጸሎት ጊዜዎች** ታዘጋጃለች፦ ጠዋት፣ የቀን መካከል፣ ምሽት፣ ከመተኛት በፊት፣ ቅዳሴ ጊዜ።

## A simple daily Rule of Life (የሕይወት ሥርዓት)

This is a **starter rule**. Adjust to your state in life. The point is consistency, not heroic length.

### 🌅 Morning offering (የጠዋት መሥዋዕት) — 5 minutes

Upon waking, before anything else:

1. **Sign of the cross.**
2. **Morning offering:**
   > «ቸር አምላኬ ሆይ፣ ይህን ቀን አንተ ዘንድ ሰጥቼዋለሁ።
   > ሁሉም ጸሎቴ፣ ሥራዬ፣ ደስታዬ፣ መከራዬ — ለአንተ ክብር ይሁን።
   > በቅዱስ ቁርባን መሥዋዕት ከኢየሱስ ጋር አንድ አደርጋቸዋለሁ።
   > በማርያም እጆች በኩል እሰጥሃለሁ።
   > አሜን።»
3. **Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be.**
4. **Brief Scripture** — the day's Gospel reading or one psalm.
5. **Petition:** name three intentions for the day.

### ☀️ Mid-day pause (የቀን መካከል) — 1 minute

When the church bells ring at noon (or any time you remember):

**The Angelus** (የሰላም መልአክ ጸሎት):

> ላኪ የእግዚአብሔር መልአክ ለማርያም በሰጣት ጊዜ —
> ከመንፈስ ቅዱስ ፀነሰች።
> ሰላም ለኪ ማርያም…
>
> «እነሆ የጌታ ባሪያ ነኝ —
> እንደ ቃልህ ይሁንልኝ።»
> ሰላም ለኪ ማርያም…
>
> ቃልም ሥጋ ሆነ —
> በመካከላችን አደረ።
> ሰላም ለኪ ማርያም…

(In Easter season, replace with the **Regina Caeli**.)

### 🍞 Grace at meals (በምግብ ላይ)

**Before:**
> «ጌታ ሆይ፣ ባርከን፣ እነዚህም ስጦታዎችህ —
> ከልግሥናህ የተቀበልነው። በክርስቶስ ጌታችን። አሜን።»

**After:**
> «ቸር አምላክ፣ ስለ ስጦታዎችህ ሁሉ እናመሰግናለን፤
> በዘላለም ሕያው የምትኖር። አሜን።»

### 🌆 Evening prayer (የምሽት ጸሎት) — 10 minutes

Before dinner or before bed:

1. **Sign of the cross.**
2. **Examination of conscience** (5 minutes) — the **Daily Examen**:
   - Thank God for the day's gifts.
   - Ask for light.
   - Review the day hour by hour: where did I love? Where did I fail?
   - Ask forgiveness for failures.
   - Resolve for tomorrow.
3. **Act of contrition:**
   > «ጌታዬ አምላኬ፣ በደሌን ሁሉ በፍጹም ልቤ እጸጸታለሁ።
   > አንተን ጨርሼ የፍቅር ጌታ ሆነህ ስለበደልኩህ —
   > ኃጢአቴን አልወድም፣ ከእንግዲህ አልሠራም።
   > በቅዱስ ጸጋህ ራሴን አስተካክላለሁ። አሜን።»
4. **Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be.**
5. **Reading** — five minutes from a Gospel, the *Imitation of Christ*, or a saint's life.

### 🌙 Before sleep (ከመተኛት በፊት) — 1 minute

> «በስመ አብ ወወልድ ወመንፈስ ቅዱስ።
> ጌታዬ ኢየሱስ፣ በእጆችህ ነፍሴን አደራ እሰጥሃለሁ።
> ቅድስት ማርያም፣ ቅዱስ ገብርኤል፣ ቅዱስ መልአኬ — በሌሊት ጠብቁኝ።
> አሜን።»

Make the sign of the cross. Sleep with peace.

## Weekly rhythm (ሳምንታዊ ሕይወት)

| Day | Practice |
|---|---|
| **Sunday** | The Lord's Day. Mass with full attention. Family meal. Rest from servile work. Visit the sick or elderly. |
| **Monday** | Pray for the Holy Souls in Purgatory. |
| **Tuesday** | Pray to your guardian angel and patron saint. |
| **Wednesday** | Pray to St. Joseph. |
| **Thursday** | Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament if possible — recall the Last Supper. |
| **Friday** | Penance day. Abstain from meat. Pray the Stations of the Cross. Recall the Passion. |
| **Saturday** | Marian day. Pray a rosary. Prepare your soul for Sunday. |

## Monthly rhythm

- **First Friday** of each month — devotion to the Sacred Heart of Jesus. Confession + Communion in reparation.
- **First Saturday** — devotion to the Immaculate Heart of Mary. Confession + Communion + rosary + 15 minutes of meditation.
- **Confession** — at least once a month even without grave sin. Spiritual maintenance.

## Annual rhythm

- **Lent** — increased fasting, almsgiving, prayer. Confession before Easter.
- **Advent** — wreath, Jesse tree, family preparation for Christmas.
- **Easter Octave** — daily celebration of the Resurrection.
- **Annual retreat** — even one day. Step out of ordinary time to listen to God.

## How to grow

**If you are starting from zero:** begin with the morning offering and one Hail Mary at night. Two minutes total. Keep it for 30 days. Then add one piece.

**If you have a stable rule:** examine which prayer has gone cold. Replace it with one slightly harder. Or add one fresh practice — adoration, daily Mass once a week, lectio divina.

**If you are advanced:** find a spiritual director. The danger of an advanced rule is pride; a director keeps it humble.

## Common mistakes

- ❌ **Long prayer when starting.** Start small. A short rule kept beats a long one abandoned.
- ❌ **Quantity over quality.** Five Hail Marys said with attention beat fifty rushed.
- ❌ **No fixed time.** "When I have time" almost always becomes "never." Pick fixed times anchored to existing routines (waking, before lunch, before bed).
- ❌ **Prayer without sacraments.** Personal prayer must be rooted in Sunday Mass and regular confession. A Christian who prays alone but never receives the sacraments is starving.
- ❌ **Stopping when dry.** Dryness is normal. The Compendium calls this *the combat of prayer* (Q574). Press on.
