## Before Mass (ከቅዳሴ በፊት)

### Preparation at home
- Dress modestly and respectfully — Mass is not casual.
- Fast for **at least one hour** from food and drink (water and medicine excepted) before receiving Communion.
- If conscious of mortal sin, **go to confession before Mass** — never receive Communion in mortal sin.

### Arriving at church
- Arrive 5–10 minutes early to settle and pray.
- Bless yourself with holy water at the entrance — recall your baptism.
- Genuflect (right knee to floor) toward the tabernacle before taking your seat.
- Kneel quietly. Pray for openness, attention, and the grace of the Mass.

## The Two Parts of the Mass (የቅዳሴ ሁለት ክፍሎች)

The Mass has two great movements:

### Part 1: Liturgy of the Word (የቃሉ ሥርዓት)

We are fed at the **table of God's word**.

| Part | What happens | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| **Entrance hymn** | Procession, sign of cross, greeting | Stand. Sing. Make the sign of the cross. |
| **Penitential Act** | Confession of unworthiness | "I confess to almighty God…" — strike the breast. |
| **Gloria** | Hymn of praise (omitted in Lent/Advent) | Sing or say with joy. |
| **Collect** | Opening prayer | Stand. Answer "Amen." |
| **First Reading** (OT) | Old Testament reading | Sit. Listen actively. |
| **Responsorial Psalm** | Sung response | Reply each verse. |
| **Second Reading** (NT) | Letter / Acts | Listen. |
| **Gospel Acclamation** | Alleluia (or Lenten verse) | Stand. |
| **Gospel** | Christ himself speaks | Trace cross on forehead, lips, heart: *"May the Lord be on my mind, my lips, my heart."* |
| **Homily** | Priest preaches | Sit. Listen. Apply to your life. |
| **Creed** | Profession of faith | Stand. Bow at "incarnate of the Virgin Mary." |
| **Prayers of the Faithful** | Intercessions | Reply "Lord, hear our prayer." |

### Part 2: Liturgy of the Eucharist (የቁርባን ሥርዓት)

We are fed at the **table of the Body and Blood of Christ**.

| Part | What happens | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| **Offertory** | Bread and wine brought; collection | Sit. Offer your work, joys, sufferings of the week with the bread and wine. |
| **Preface and Sanctus** | "Holy, Holy, Holy…" | Stand. Join the angels' hymn. |
| **Eucharistic Prayer** | The great central prayer | Kneel. **Adore.** This is the heart of the Mass — the consecration. |
| **Consecration** | "This is my body… this is my blood." | Bow your head. The bread and wine **become** Christ's Body and Blood. |
| **Memorial Acclamation** | "We proclaim your death…" | Reply with the people. |
| **Doxology and Great Amen** | "Through him, with him, in him…" | Reply with full voice: "Amen!" |
| **Our Father** | Lord's Prayer | Stand. Pray with confidence. |
| **Sign of Peace** | Greet neighbors | Offer peace simply and reverently. |
| **Lamb of God** | "Lamb of God, who takes away…" | Strike the breast. |
| **Communion** | Reception of the Eucharist | If properly disposed, go forward. (See [[teaching/practical-communion]].) |
| **Prayer after Communion** | Thanksgiving | Stand. |
| **Final Blessing & Dismissal** | "Go in peace." | Make the sign of the cross. Reply "Thanks be to God." |

## Postures (የተገቢ አቀማመጦች)

| Posture | When |
|---|---|
| **Standing** | Entrance, Gospel, Creed, Eucharistic Prayer (some places), final blessing |
| **Sitting** | Readings, Psalm, Homily, Offertory, after Communion |
| **Kneeling** | Eucharistic Prayer (consecration), before Communion, after Communion |
| **Bowing** | At the holy name of Jesus, at "incarnate of the Virgin Mary" in the Creed |
| **Sign of the Cross** | Entry, Penitential Act, before Gospel (small crosses), final blessing |

## Active Participation (ንቁ ተሳትፎ)

The Second Vatican Council taught that participation must be **conscious, active, and full**. This is not the same as being noisy or busy. It means:

- **Conscious** — knowing what is happening at each part of the Mass.
- **Active** — singing, responding, making the gestures, listening.
- **Full** — uniting your heart with what Christ is offering on the altar — and offering yourself with him.

The most important "activity" is **interior**: offer your whole life to the Father with Christ's sacrifice.

## After Mass (ከቅዳሴ በኋላ)

- Don't rush out. Spend a few minutes in thanksgiving — Christ is dwelling in you sacramentally.
- The Eucharist is meant to be carried into the world. Live what you received.
- Make a Sunday a day of rest, family, charity. The whole day belongs to the Lord.

## Common mistakes to avoid

- ❌ Arriving after the Gospel and counting it as Sunday Mass (you have not fulfilled the Sunday obligation).
- ❌ Receiving Communion in mortal sin without confession.
- ❌ Leaving immediately after Communion — wait for the final blessing.
- ❌ Treating the church like a public space — phones, conversation, casual movement.
- ❌ Coming dressed as for the beach or sport.
