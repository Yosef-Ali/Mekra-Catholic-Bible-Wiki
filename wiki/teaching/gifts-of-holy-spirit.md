## The two lists

The Catholic tradition distinguishes:

- **Seven Gifts** (በስጦታ) — the Holy Spirit's *permanent dispositions* implanted in the soul. Roots.
- **Twelve Fruits** (ፍሬ) — the Holy Spirit's *visible results* in the life of one who lives by the Spirit. Branches and fruit.

The gifts are received especially at **baptism** and strengthened at **confirmation (ሜሮን)**. The fruits ripen wherever the gifts are watered by prayer and obedience.

## The Seven Gifts of the Holy Spirit

Drawn from Isaiah 11:2–3, the prophecy of the Spirit who would rest upon the Messiah:

> «የእግዚአብሔር መንፈስ — የጥበብና የማስተዋል መንፈስ — የምክርና የኃይል መንፈስ — የእውቀትና የእግዚአብሔር ፍርሃት መንፈስ — በላዩ ላይ ያርፋል።»
> — ኢሳ. 11:2–3

| # | Gift | Amharic | What it does |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Wisdom** (Sapientia) | ጥበብ | To love God above all and judge all things in light of him |
| 2 | **Understanding** (Intellectus) | ማስተዋል | To penetrate truths of the faith with insight |
| 3 | **Counsel** (Consilium) | ምክር | To choose rightly in difficult moral situations |
| 4 | **Fortitude / Courage** (Fortitudo) | ኃይል / ድፍረት | To stand firm in the face of opposition or fear |
| 5 | **Knowledge** (Scientia) | እውቀት | To see created things rightly — neither despising them nor making idols of them |
| 6 | **Piety** (Pietas) | ሃይማኖት / ታማኝነት | Filial love for God as Father and reverence for sacred things |
| 7 | **Fear of the Lord** (Timor Domini) | የእግዚአብሔር ፍርሃት | Holy reverence — not slavish fear, but the love that hates to grieve God |

These are not seven different "personalities" the Spirit has. They are seven different **modes** by which the one Spirit operates in the soul, fitting it for the various seasons of Christian life.

## How they work

The **theological virtues** (faith, hope, charity) are how *we* respond to God. The **gifts of the Spirit** are how *the Spirit* moves us beyond what natural virtue can do.

A person of natural virtue acts wisely **on his own initiative**. A person living by the gifts is **moved by the Spirit** — sometimes in ways that surpass natural reasoning. Saints speak of this as being "carried" — the soul like a sailboat, the Spirit like the wind.

## When are the gifts increased?

- **At baptism** — the seed is planted.
- **At confirmation** — the seal of the Spirit, given for spiritual maturity.
- **At every reception of the Eucharist worthily**.
- **Through prayer, fasting, and almsgiving** — the soul becomes more receptive.
- **Through suffering well** — the Spirit purifies the soul that does not run from the cross.

## The Twelve Fruits of the Holy Spirit

Saint Paul's list (ገላ. 5:22–23):

> «የመንፈስ ፍሬ ግን ፍቅር ነው — ደስታ — ሰላም — ትዕግሥት — ቸርነት — በጎነት — ታማኝነት — ገርነት — ራስን መግዛት።»

The Latin Vulgate and the Catholic tradition expand to twelve:

| # | Fruit | Amharic | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Charity / Love** | ፍቅር | Love of God and neighbor for God's sake |
| 2 | **Joy** | ደስታ | Spiritual gladness rooted in God, not circumstance |
| 3 | **Peace** | ሰላም | The tranquility of order — not absence of conflict, but rightness with God |
| 4 | **Patience** | ትዕግሥት | Endurance under trial without resentment |
| 5 | **Kindness** | ቸርነት | Active goodwill toward all |
| 6 | **Goodness** | በጎነት | Habitual integrity of soul |
| 7 | **Generosity** | ልግሥና | Freedom from grasping; openness to give |
| 8 | **Gentleness / Meekness** | ገርነት | Strength under control; the bull's strength yoked to the plow |
| 9 | **Faithfulness** | ታማኝነት | Reliability in the small and the large |
| 10 | **Modesty** | ትሕትና / ራስን ማነስ | Right ordering of self-presentation |
| 11 | **Self-control / Continence** | ራስን መግዛት | Mastery over the appetites |
| 12 | **Chastity** | ንጽሕና | Right ordering of sexual desire to love |

## How to read the fruits

The fruits are **diagnostic**. If we want to know whether the Spirit is at work in a soul, look for these. **Spiritual gifts without fruit are suspect.** A person who claims great visions but cannot keep peace with his family lacks the basic test of true spirituality.

> «ከፍሬዎቻቸው ታውቋቸዋላችሁ።»
> — ማቴ. 7:16

The Reformer Martin Luther himself acknowledged: *"It is not enough to be Christian by name. The fruits show whether the tree is alive."*

## Praying to the Holy Spirit

The traditional **Veni Sancte Spiritus** prayer — Come, Holy Spirit — should be on the lips of every Catholic when about to make a difficult decision, to pray seriously, or to teach the faith:

> «ና — መንፈስ ቅዱስ — ና።
> ከሰማይ ላክ — የብርሃንህ ጨረር።
> ና — የድሆች አባት — ና።
> ና — የስጦታ ለጋሽ — ና።
> የነፍሳችን ብርሃን ና።
>
> ቸር አጽናኝ — ጣፋጭ የነፍስ እንግዳ — ጣፋጭ ንፋስ።
> በጭንቀት ሰዓት — ዕረፍት — በሥራ ወቅት ቅዝቃዜ — በለቅሶ ጊዜ ምክር።
>
> በጣም ብሩህ ብርሃን ሆይ — የልባችንን ጥልቅ ሙላ።
> ከጸጋህ ውጭ — በሰው ውስጥ ምንም ንጹሕ የለም።
> ደካማውን ፈውስ — የቆሰለውን ጠብቅ።
> ጠንካሮችን አጥብቅ — የቀዘቀዙትን አንቀሳቅስ — የተቅበዘበዙትን ምራ።
>
> በአንተ ለሚታመኑ ሁሉ — ቅዱሳት ስጦታዎችን ስጥ።
> የበጎነት ሽልማት — የመጨረሻ ድል — የዘላለም ደስታ — ስጥ።
> አሜን — አሌሉያ።»
