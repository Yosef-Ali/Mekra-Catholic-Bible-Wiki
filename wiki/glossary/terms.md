# Glossary — Amharic Catholic Terminology

**Type:** glossary
**Last updated:** 2026-04-09

| Amharic | English | Context |
|---|---|---|
| ጥምቀት | Baptism | First sacrament of initiation |
| ሜሮን | Chrismation / Confirmation | Sacrament of strengthening in the Spirit |
| ቅዱስ ቁርባን | Holy Eucharist | Body and Blood of Christ; central sacrament |
| ንስሐ | Penance / Reconciliation | Sacrament of forgiveness of sins |
| ቅብዐ ቅዱስ | Anointing of the Sick | Sacrament for the seriously ill |
| ክህነት | Holy Orders / Priesthood | Sacrament of ordained ministry |
| ተክሊል | Matrimony / Marriage | Sacrament of marital union |
| ቤተክርስቲያን | Church | The Body of Christ; community of the faithful |
| ሐዋርያዊ ትውፊት | Apostolic Tradition | Transmission of faith from the Apostles |
| መጽሐፍ ቅዱስ | Holy Scripture / Bible | The written Word of God |
| ቀኖና | Canon | Official list of inspired books of Scripture |
| ቅዳሴ | Qiddase / Mass / Divine Liturgy | Eucharistic liturgical celebration |
| ሥርዓተ አምልኮ | Liturgy | Public worship of the Church |
| ኑዛዜ | Confession | Verbal confession of sins to a priest |
| ካህን | Priest | Ordained minister of sacraments |
| ጳጳስ | Bishop | Successor of the Apostles; head of a diocese |
| ዲያቆን | Deacon | Ordained minister of service |
| ምእመናን | The Faithful / Laity | Baptized members of the Church |
| ሐዋርያ | Apostle | One sent by Christ; the Twelve and their successors |
| ወንጌል | Gospel | The Good News; the four Gospels |
| ቃለ እግዚአብሔር | Word of God | Scripture and divine revelation |
| መሥዋዕት | Sacrifice | Christ's sacrifice; the Eucharistic sacrifice |
| ፋሲካ | Pascha / Easter | Christ's Passover; the central Christian feast |
| ዕርገት | Ascension | Christ's bodily ascent into heaven |
| ጰንጠቆስጤ | Pentecost | Descent of the Holy Spirit on the Apostles |
